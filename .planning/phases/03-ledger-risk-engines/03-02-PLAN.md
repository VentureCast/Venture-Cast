<objective>
Build the PURE 3-tier risk engine for the AMM: a single `evaluate(trade, snapshot, tierConfig)`
that returns an allow/reject decision plus, on rejection, a typed RiskError and a RiskEvent draft.

The engine is 100% pure — NO Mongoose, NO DB, NO I/O. All inputs (current reserve, user position,
daily volume, market status, reference/new price, tier caps) are passed IN by the caller. The Phase 4
orchestrator queries those values; risk stays a pure decision function so it is trivially unit-testable.

Purpose: RISK-01..06 — per-tier max trade size, per-user/per-market max position, daily volume cap,
reserve-floor invariant, dynamic sell cap, and circuit breaker (paused + price-move threshold).
Output: services/amm/risk/{checks,index}.js + tests/unit/risk.test.js
</objective>
